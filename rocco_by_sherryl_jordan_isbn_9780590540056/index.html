Rocco by Sherryl Jordan

Title: Rocco
Author: Sherryl Jordan

Review: Rocco is a heartwarming and adventurous children’s book written by Sherryl Jordan. The story follows a young boy named Rocco who lives in a small Italian village with his family. Rocco dreams of becoming a great artist, but his father wants him to follow in his footsteps and become a fisherman. When Rocco’s father falls ill, Rocco must make a difficult decision - should he stay and help his family or follow his dreams?

The book is beautifully illustrated and has a captivating storyline that will keep young readers engaged. The target age group for this book is 7-10 years old, as the story contains important life lessons that are appropriate for this age range.

Some of the life lessons covered in the book include the importance of family, following your dreams, and making difficult decisions. These themes are woven seamlessly into the story, making it an enjoyable and educational read for young children.

Personal thoughts, I highly recommend Rocco to parents, teachers, and young readers alike. It is a wonderful book that teaches valuable life lessons while also being entertaining and engaging. So, go ahead and buy Rocco - you won’t be disappointed!
