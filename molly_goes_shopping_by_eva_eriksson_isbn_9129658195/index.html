Molly Goes Shopping by Eva Eriksson

Title: Molly Goes Shopping
Author: Eva Eriksson

Molly Goes Shopping is a delightful children’s book written by Eva Eriksson, aimed at children between the ages of 4 to 8 years old. The story follows the adventures of a young girl named Molly, who goes shopping with her mother and learns important life lessons along the way.

The illustrations in the book are vibrant and engaging, capturing the attention of young readers. The story is simple and easy to understand, making it perfect for early readers or as a bedtime story.

One of the key life lessons covered in the book is the importance of being responsible and taking care of one’s belongings. Molly loses her purse during the shopping trip and has to retrace her steps to find it. This teaches children the value of being mindful of their possessions and the consequences of not doing so.

Another important lesson in the book is the concept of patience and waiting for one’s turn. Molly has to wait in line at the store, and this teaches children the importance of being patient and respecting others’ time.

My reccomendation, Molly Goes Shopping is a wonderful children’s book that not only entertains but also teaches valuable life lessons. I highly recommend this book for children between the ages of 4 to 8 years old. It would make a great addition to any child’s library and would be a perfect gift for young readers.
