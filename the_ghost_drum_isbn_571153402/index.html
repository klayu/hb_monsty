The Ghost Drum

Title: The Ghost Drum
Author: Susan Price

The Ghost Drum is a captivating children’s book written by Susan Price, which takes readers on a magical journey through a world filled with mythical creatures and enchanting adventures. This book is an excellent choice for children aged 8 to 12 years old, as it offers a perfect blend of fantasy, mystery, and suspense.

The story revolves around a young girl named Siri, who is chosen by the mythical creatures known as the Huldrefolk to become their champion. The Huldrefolk are in danger, and it is up to Siri to save them from the evil queen, Skadi. Throughout her adventure, Siri learns about the importance of bravery, friendship, and self-belief.

The Ghost Drum is an excellent book for children as it not only provides an entertaining and engaging story but also teaches valuable life lessons. Some of the life lessons covered in the book include:

  1. Courage: Siri must face her fears and stand up against the evil queen, Skadi, to save the Huldrefolk. This teaches children the importance of bravery and standing up for what is right.
  2. Friendship: Siri forms strong bonds with the mythical creatures she encounters on her journey. This emphasizes the importance of building strong friendships and working together as a team.
  3. Self-belief: Siri initially doubts her abilities but learns to trust in herself and her newfound powers. This teaches children the importance of self-confidence and believing in their own abilities.

    I highly recommend The Ghost Drum for children aged 8 to 12 years old. It is a delightful and enchanting book that will keep young readers engaged while also teaching them valuable life lessons. So, go ahead and buy this book for your child, and let them embark on a magical adventure with Siri and the Huldrefolk!
