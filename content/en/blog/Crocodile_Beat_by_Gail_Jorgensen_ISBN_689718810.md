Crocodile Beat is a delightful children's book written by Gail Jorgensen, which takes the reader on an adventurous journey into the world of a young crocodile named Croc. The book is targeted towards children between the ages of 4 to 8 years old and is filled with vibrant illustrations that capture the imagination of young readers.</br></br>
The story follows Croc as he learns to play the drums and eventually forms a band with his animal friends. Throughout the book, Croc and his friends encounter various challenges, such as dealing with stage fright and overcoming disagreements within the band. Overall, Crocodile Beat is a wonderful children's book that not only entertains but also educates. I would highly recommend this book for children between the ages of 4 to 8 years old, as it provides a fun and engaging way to learn valuable life lessons.
