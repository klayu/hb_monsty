Not Chicage, Not Here by Kirsten Boie

Title: Not Chicago, Not Here
Author: Kirsten Boie

“Not Chicago, Not Here” is a heartwarming children’s book by Kirsten Boie that tells the story of a young girl named Lena, who struggles with the idea of moving to a new city and leaving her friends behind. The book beautifully captures the emotions and challenges that children face when dealing with change and uncertainty.

The target age group for this book is 8 to 12 years old. The relatable characters and engaging storyline make it an enjoyable read for children in this age range. The book also offers valuable life lessons that can help children understand the importance of friendship, resilience, and adapting to new situations.

In addition to being an entertaining read, “Not Chicago, Not Here” teaches children the importance of embracing change and finding joy in new experiences. The book also highlights the power of friendship and the value of supportive relationships.

Overall, I would highly recommend “Not Chicago, Not Here” for children aged 8 to 12. The book is a wonderful blend of entertainment and life lessons, making it a valuable addition to any child’s library.
