Fray Perico and His Donkey by Juan Munoz

Title: Fray Perico and His Donkey
Author: Juan Munoz

Review: Fray Perico and His Donkey is a delightful children’s book that follows the adventures of Fray Perico, a kind-hearted monk, and his loyal donkey. The story takes place in a small village where Fray Perico and his donkey become beloved figures due to their generosity and helpful nature.

The book is aimed at children between the ages of 5 and 8 years old. The colorful illustrations and simple language make it an engaging read for young readers. The story also teaches valuable life lessons such as the importance of kindness, empathy, and hard work.

Fray Perico and His Donkey is a heartwarming tale that will capture the hearts of young readers. I highly recommend this book for parents and educators looking for a charming story that teaches important values in a fun and engaging way.

Buy Recommendation: Yes, I would definitely recommend this book for children aged 5 to 8 years old.
