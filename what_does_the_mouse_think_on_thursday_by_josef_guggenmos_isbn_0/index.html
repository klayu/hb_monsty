What Does the Mouse Think on Thursday by Josef Guggenmos

Title: “What Does the Mouse Think on Thursday”
Author: Josef Guggenmos

Review: “What Does the Mouse Think on Thursday” is a charming children’s book that follows the adventures of a curious little mouse named Tuffy. The story takes place over the course of a week, with each day bringing new discoveries and experiences for Tuffy. The book is written in a simple and engaging manner, making it perfect for young readers.

The target age group for this book is children between the ages of 4 and 8. The illustrations are vibrant and eye-catching, which will capture the attention of young readers. The story also teaches important life lessons such as the importance of friendship, embracing new experiences, and being kind to others.

Overall, “What Does the Mouse Think on Thursday” is a delightful book that will not only entertain young readers but also teach them valuable life lessons. I highly recommend this book for parents, teachers, and caregivers looking for a fun and educational read for their children.
