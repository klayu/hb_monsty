Title: Lavender’s Blue
Author: Kathleen Lines

Review: Lavender’s Blue is a charming and heartwarming children’s book that captures the essence of childhood innocence and imagination. The story follows the adventures of a young girl named Lavender, who embarks on a magical journey through the countryside, encountering various animals and learning valuable life lessons along the way.

The book is beautifully illustrated, with vibrant colors and intricate details that bring the story to life. The characters are lovable and relatable, making it easy for young readers to connect with them. The story itself is engaging and keeps the reader’s attention from beginning to end.
