A Dog So Small by Philippa Pearce

Title: A Dog So Small
Author: Philippa Pearce

Review: A Dog So Small is a heartwarming and enchanting children’s book that captivates the imagination of young readers. Written by the talented author Philippa Pearce, this story follows the adventures of a young boy named Ben and his encounter with a magical, tiny dog.
Target Age Group: This book is primarily aimed at children between the ages of 7 and 10 years old. The simple yet engaging language and the relatable themes make it an ideal read for this age group.
Life Lessons: A Dog So Small touches upon several valuable life lessons, including the importance of friendship, empathy, and responsibility. The story also highlights the power of imagination and the value of cherishing the small things in life.
Recommendation: I would highly recommend A Dog So Small to parents, teachers, and young readers alike. It is a charming tale that will captivate the hearts of children and leave them with a sense of wonder and joy. So, go ahead and buy this delightful book to embark on a magical journey with Ben and his tiny dog!
